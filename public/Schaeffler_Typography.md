# Schaeffler Typography Guidelines

## Font Family

**Arial** — used for all digital applications.

Chosen for compatibility across a wide variety of devices, operating systems, and browsers to guarantee correct display and legibility.

---

## Type Scale

| Level | Size | Line Spacing | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | 36px | 43px | Regular |
| H2 | 28px | 35px | Regular |
| H3 | 20px | 26px | Regular |
| H4 | 18px | 25px | **Bold** |
| H5 | 18px | 25px | Regular |
| H6 | 16px | 25px | **Bold** |
| Copy / Body | 16px | 25px | Regular |
| Meta / Labels / Captions | 13px | 20px | Regular |

---

## Rules

- Font must be compatible with a variety of devices, operating systems, and browsers.
- Do not substitute Arial with other fonts in digital or application contexts.
